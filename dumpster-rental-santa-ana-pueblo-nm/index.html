Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the typical rental period for your dumpsters?

Our standard rental period is 7 to 10 days nationwide. We understand project timelines can vary, so we offer flexible extensions upon request to accommodate your needs

2. How do I know if I need a 20-yard or 30-yard dumpster?

Consider the scale of your debris. A 20-yard dumpster is great for 3-4 rooms of furniture or a single-layer roof. Choose a 30-yard for larger whole-home cleanouts, additions, or multi-story projects.

3. Can you deliver a dumpster to a home with a narrow driveway?

Yes, we service properties with tight access nationwide. Our drivers are experienced in careful placement. Please inform us of any access restrictions (like overhead wires or narrow gates) when you book

4. What is not allowed in your rental dumpsters?

Hazardous materials are strictly prohibited. This includes paints, chemicals, oils, batteries, tires, and appliances with refrigerants. Ashes and medical waste are also not accepted.

Santa Ana Pueblo, NM Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Santa Ana Pueblo (Eastern Keres: Tamaya ) is a census-designated place (CDP) in Sandoval County, New Mexico, United States. As of the 2000 census, the CDP had a total population of 479. It is part of the Albuquerque Metropolitan Statistical Area. The Pueblo, named Tamaya in Keres, administers a total reservation land of 73,000 acres (295 km2) in the Rio Grande valley and is composed of Native Americans who speak an eastern dialect of the Keresan languages.
